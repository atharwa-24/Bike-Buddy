About us

What is Bike Buddy?

Bike Buddy is an inexpensive and simple solution to safe riding and quick response to accident locations. This project aims to save as many lives as possible.

How we built it?

We used a few piezo sensors strategically placed at places that would feel the pressure after being hit during an accident, a neo-6m GPS module and a NodeMCU.

What is our future?

In the future, Bike Buddy can be utilized to understand the happenings before a crash and to better understand which roads, speeds, etc. are more prone to accidents.
